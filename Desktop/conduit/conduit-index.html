Common questions

Is Conduit really free?
+

Yes. The free tier includes 100 AI calls/month, 500 MB storage, and full access to all 15 AI tools. No credit card required.

What is an AI call?
+

One AI call = one generation request to Claude. Chat messages, article generation, brief building, repurposing, scoring — any feature that calls the AI. Usage is shown in the topbar credit meter at all times.

Do I need to migrate my existing CMS?
+

No. Prism works as a standalone CMS or as a plugin layer on Strapi, WordPress, Sanity, or Contentful. Install the plugin, connect your Claude API key, and 15 AI tools appear inside your existing CMS admin.

Can I use my own Claude API key?
+

Yes. Prism uses your Claude API key directly from your browser. It goes straight to Anthropic — never to Prism servers. You control costs, Prism tracks usage against your plan limits.

What happens when I hit my AI call limit?
+

By default, further AI calls are blocked with a clear upgrade/purchase link. You can enable auto-purchase in Settings to automatically buy add-on packs when you approach the limit.

Is the content I create owned by me?
+

100% yes. All content you create belongs to you. Prism does not claim ownership. You can export everything as JSON from Settings at any time.

Pricing questions

Can I change plans at any time?
+

Yes. Upgrade instantly, downgrade at billing cycle end. Pro-rated credits on upgrade.

What counts as an AI call?
+

Any request that calls Claude: chat message, article generation, brief, repurposing, scoring — every generation action. One request = one call.

Do unused credits roll over?
+

On Free and Pro plans, credits reset monthly and do not roll over. On Business, purchased add-on packs roll over once. Always shown clearly before purchase.

Do you store my Claude API key?
+

Your key is stored in your browser's localStorage only. It goes directly from your browser to the Anthropic API — never through Prism servers.
